Privacy & Security

Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your personal information. All data is stored in secure servers within India and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

All payment transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways. We do not store your UPI PIN or card details on our servers.

You have full control over your data. You can request a copy of your data, update it, or delete your account at any time through your dashboard settings.

Transparency

We clearly communicate how your data is used. No hidden tracking, no selling of data. We only use your information to provide and improve our services.

Security Measures

Our platform includes: Two-factor authentication, automatic session timeout, suspicious activity alerts, regular security audits, and 24/7 monitoring.
